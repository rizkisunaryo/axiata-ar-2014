Supun Weerasinghe
Managing Director and Chief Executive Officer
Robi Axiata Limited

Supun Weerasinghe is the Managing Director and Chief Executive Officer of Robi. Prior to joining Robi, Supun served as Chief Strategy Officer of Axiata Group. He had also served as the Head of Network Transformation Strategic Business Unit under which he provided leadership to Group Technology, Carrier Collaboration and Axiata Intelligence Unit (previously Axiata Management Services). During this period, Supun assisted Axiata Group to craft its long-term strategic plan.

He has an MBA from the University of Western Sydney, Australia and is an alumnus of the Harvard Business School (AMP 182).
