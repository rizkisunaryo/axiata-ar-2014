Hasnul Suhaimi
President Director
PT XL Axiata Tbk

Hasnul obtained his Bachelor’s degree in Electrical Engineering from Bandung Institute of Technology (ITB) in 1981 and Master of Business Administration degree from the University of Hawaii,USA, in 1992. He has held various directorship positions in several national telecommunication companies such as PT Indosat Multi Media Mobile (IM3) (2001-2002) as President Director and PT Telekomunikasi Seluler (Telkomsel) as Director of Commerce (1998-2002) prior to joining XL in 2006. He is currently also President Commissioner of PT XL Planet Digital since 8 July 2013 and Director of Robi Axiata Limited since 6 November 2012

Under his leadership, XL has transformed to become the second largest cellular provider in Indonesia and has been recognised as one of Asia’s Best Managed Companies 2009 by Euromoney magazine for Overall Most Convincing and Coherent Strategy, Best Emerging Markets Carrier in 17th Annual Telecom Asia Awards 2014, and The Most Innovative Telecom Service Provider of the Year from Frost & Sullivan 2014 Asia Pacific ICT Awards.

In 2014, Hasnul was awarded as Most Inspirational Chief Executive Officer from Men’s Obsession Award 2014 and Satyalancana Wira Karya, a special honors from The President of Republic Indonesia for his services in providing devotion to the state and nation of Indonesia.
