Tan Gim Boon
Group General Counsel and Risk Officer

Gim graduated with a Bachelor of Commerce in 1993 and a Bachelor of Laws in 1995 from University of Adelaide, Australia. In 2000, he completed a Master of Laws from University of New South Wales, Australia. Gim was admitted as an Advocate and Solicitor of the High Court of Malaya in 1997 and admitted as a solicitor in New South Wales, Australia in 2002.

He joined TMI (now Axiata) in 2004. Prior to joining Axiata, he was working as a lawyer in Malaysia and Australia. His areas of practice were predominantly in the fields of mergers and acquisitions, equity capital markets and corporate finance. Gim’s last post before joining Axiata, was with Malaysia’s largest law firm, Zaid Ibrahim & Co.
