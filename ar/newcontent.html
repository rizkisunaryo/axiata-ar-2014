PROFILE OF OPERATING COMPANIES MANAGEMENT TEAM
Get to konw the people that drive exceptional performance across the Group

CELCOM
Celcom is Malaysia’s premier and most experienced mobile telecommunications company with the widest coverage in the nation

XL AXIATA
XL is one of Indonesia’s leading telecommunications service providers and offers an array of innovative telecommunications products and services ranging from voice, SMS and VAS to mobile data covering more than 90% of the population

ROBI
Robi, the most dynamic and rapidly growing telecommunications operator in Bangladesh, is developing its services to meet increasing customer needs ranging from voice and high speed Internet services to tailor- made telecommunications solutions

DIALOG
Dialog has been at the forefront of innovation in the mobile industry in Sri Lanka since the late 90’s, driving the country’s mobile telephony infrastructure to a level of advancement on par with the developed world

SMART
Smart is Cambodia’s second largest mobile telecommunications company serving over 6.5 million subscribers

M1
M1 is Singapore’s most vibrant and dynamic communications company, providing mobile and fixed services to close to 2 million customers

IDEA
Idea is one of the top three mobile operators in India, with an annual revenue of USD5 billion and revenue market share of over 17%. With over 150 million subscribers, Idea ranks sixth in global rankings of operators in subscriber terms, for a single country operations

MULTINET
Multinet is a leading independent telecommunications solution provider in Pakistan, operating a nationwide optical fibre cable network, connecting the major cities across Pakistan

EDOTCO
edotco Group was set-up in 2013, to own and manage the Axiata group of companies passive network infrastructure assets. The company has made significant progress in 2014, growing its tower portfolio

AXIATA DIGITAL SERVICES
Axiata established its digital services arm, Axiata Digital Services to place the Group in the same space as OTT service providers, enabling Axiata to expand its portfolio of services to cater to online customers on smartphones, tablets and fixed Internet
