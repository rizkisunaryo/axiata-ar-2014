Talent Development and Unleashing Potential

The Axiata Talent Development Programme which includes a joint programme with INSEAD (facilitated by INSEAD at its campus in Singapore); a Telco mini-MBA programme; and a ‘War Game’ business simulation programme, continued to strengthen and groom our employees in 2014. The Axiata Group and OpCo Accelerated Programmes (GADP and OADP) enabled high performers to accept challenges at the next level. More detailed information on our training programmes can be accessed at XXX

In 2014, the Group Talent management and development process of accelerated development, went through a process of improvement and decentralisation. This change was made in order to increase the interaction between selected employees and their respective superiors in each country. Specific training programmes were conducted to facilitate this change in the Talent Management Programme.

Last year a new process was introduced to facilitate the transition of top management into new roles at the CXO level. This includes a transition plan covering the first 100 days for each CXO level movement. This transition plan identifies the key factors and initiatives for the CXO to address and consider during his or her first 100 days. These improvements were made to enhance the capacity of top management and to test new talents within a challenging environment.
